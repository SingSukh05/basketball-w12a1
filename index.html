BASKETBALL

About the Sport

Basketball is a fast-paced team sport played on a rectangular court where two teams of five players compete to score by shooting a ball through the opponent's hoop. Invented by Dr. James Naismith in 1891, the game has grown into one of the world's most popular sports, with the NBA serving as its premier professional league. Known for its athleticism, strategy, and electrifying moments, basketball continues to captivate millions of fans across the globe.
